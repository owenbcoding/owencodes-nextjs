## My daily tools

- **Editor**: Cursor, with a tight feedback loop between prompts and diffs.
- **Terminal**: A Zsh setup with fast prompt and fuzzy history.
- **Docs**: Inline AI answers next to first-party documentation.

## Tips that saved me hours

1. Learn your editor's keybindings first, not later.
2. Script the boring stuff once, reuse it forever.
3. Use a consistent formatter and linter so reviews focus on logic, not style.

## Keep learning

Tooling evolves fast. Revisit your setup every few months and drop what no longer earns its keep.
